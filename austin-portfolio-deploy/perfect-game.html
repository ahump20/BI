South Texas Sliders

Founded in 2003 in San Antonio, the South Texas Sliders have established themselves as a premier travel organization. With high-level instruction and multiple teams, they've produced numerous college commitments across Texas universities.

Est. 2003 San Antonio College Commitments
Austin Wings

Formed in 1999 to operate a first-class program for worthy youth in Austin and Central Texas, the Wings offer high-level instruction and a competitive environment that promotes life skills and community leadership. Their teams have earned national titles and top finishes across Premier and Triple Crown events.

Est. 1999 Austin/Central TX National Titles
Houston Banditos

Established in 1994 and based in Tomball, the Banditos Baseball Club is one of the nation's most successful programs, with over 280 MLB drafted alumni and more than 1,400 college commitments. The Banditos achieved an unprecedented sweep in 2016, winning the 15U PG World Series, WWBA National Championship, BCS National Championship, and Freshman World Championship.

Est. 1994 280+ MLB Drafted 1,400+ College 2016 Grand Slam
Dulin's Dodgers

Created in 2000, this elite program in the Southeast fields teams for athletes aged 8–18. Dulins Dodgers have won 16 national championships and placed in nine other national tournaments, producing 15 Major League players, eight first-round draft picks and over 700 college scholarships.

Est. 2000 15 MLB Players 8 First Round 16 Championships
Germantown Baseball Club

A tradition-rich program based in Germantown, Tennessee. The club provides opportunities for boys aged 7–14 to play competitive baseball under top instructors, competing at the AAA level within USSSA rules.
